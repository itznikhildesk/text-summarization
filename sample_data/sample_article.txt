Sustainable AI is an emerging field that focuses on the environmental impact of artificial intelligence. As models grow larger, their energy consumption and carbon footprint increase significantly. 

GreenSumm aims to address this by promoting "Green AI" - AI that is efficient and environmentally friendly. By using techniques like knowledge distillation and quantization, we can create models that perform well on mobile devices while consuming a fraction of the power.

This project demonstrates that we don't always need the largest models to get useful results, especially in resource-constrained environments like mobile phones or IoT edge devices.
